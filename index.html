ABOUT IEEE STUDENT'S BRANCH GHRCE

Biography

Founded in December 1999 with initially 44 members, the students chapter of IEEE has come along a long way. The efforts and dedication towards the smallest of tasks under the IEEE banner has lead to the chapter being recognized as one of the best chapters in the Bombay(Mumbai) division. With efforts from both the subsequent executive committees and the institute, IEEE SB GHRCE has made its mark on the international IEEE map in a very short span of a decade. Be it winning various international competitions or organizing international conferences or occupying positions of importance in various IEEE bodies, IEEE SB GHRCE truly makes us feel proud of our success. It gives us immense pleasure to inform all that within a span of 1 year we have almost increased our membership count by nine times!! In 2012, the membership count was 105, but with dedicated efforts of the entire execom, the current count of membership stands at 1889!! With this, it makes us Asia’s largest student branch!! The detailed membership list can be seen here. However, this is just the beginning. Regards, The IEEE Student Branch, GHRCE.

BY THE CHAIRS

It is a great privilege and an honour to serve as Chairperson of the IEEE Student Branch of G. H. Raisoni college of Engineering( GHRCE ), Nagpur. Over the years, IEEE SB GHRCE being the largest student branch in Asia- Pacific has grown to become a leading SB of India council that attracts the best and brightest students from across the country. The alumni of this institution connected with this SB are sought after by leading companies from across the world. I joined IEEE during my first year and got a lot of opportunities to express, explore and expand my knowledge in various fields. IEEE is an organization which helps students and other organization's scope of interest to expand into numerous fields. It provides students a platform to volunteer,and showcase their skills.

Akash Raut

Chair, SB-GHRCE
IEEE is the world's largest technical and professional society which helps the one willing to work get recognition and also get rewarded. Being the Chair of IEEE GHRCE CS Chapter, I got the opportunity to work with the best team of execoms and volunteers. Here, I realized that the number of events you conduct, or the number of students participating is immaterial. What matters is how will the participants get benefited from it technically and is the event really worth attending. Not only technically, IEEE helps you to grow as a better human aiming to turn society a better place. There are various chapters, affinity groups and special interest groups for this. Also, every year, each Student branch makes sure to conduct a humanitarian project for helping the society.

Savneet Chhatwal

CS Chapter Chair
I started my IEEE journey 3 years ago in college and really didn’t know where it would take me. I knew that joining would help me to develop my skills and knowledge. IEEE-HKN is the Honor Society of IEEE, Membership in IEEE-HKN not only recognizes academic achievements but also indicates leadership potential while the course of a professional career. there is a broader purpose as considered by its founders to assist its Members throughout their lives in becoming better professionals as well as better citizens. The two critical components of being an engineer are keeping awake with front line development and team of best people , What I think IEEE gives is the best blend of the two system. I believe professionals in technology fields should join IEEE.
