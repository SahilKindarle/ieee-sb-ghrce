IEEE Student Branch

G. H. Raisoni College of Engineering 

Founded in December 1999 with initially 44 members, the students chapter of IEEE has come along a long way. The efforts and dedication towards the smallest of tasks under the IEEE banner has lead to the chapter being recognized as one of the best chapters in the Bombay(Mumbai) division.

With efforts from both the subsequent executive committees and the institute, IEEE SB GHRCE has made its mark on the international IEEE map in a very short span of  a decade. Be it winning various international competitions or organizing international conferences or occupying positions of importance in various IEEE bodies, IEEE SB GHRCE truly makes us feel proud of our success.

It gives us immense pleasure to inform all that within a span of 1 year we have almost increased our membership count by nine times!! In 2012, the membership count was 105, but with dedicated efforts of the entire execom, the current count of membership stands at 1889!! With this, it makes us Asia’s largest student branch!! The detailed membership list can be seen here.

However, this is just the beginning. This year, after visiting places across the globe, IEEE SB GHRCE’s very own international conference – International Conference in Emerging Trends in Engineering & Technology (ICETET) – is coming back to the place of it’s origin – NAGPUR. Going to be held in December 2013 it is one of the renowned and anticipated IEEE conferences across the world. The conference is now in its 6th edition -previously being held at Nagpur, Goa, Mauritius and Japan.

For more details on the events to be conducted, the membership procedures, et al… visit this website regularly.

Regards,
The IEEE Student Branch, GHRCE.
